About AI Patents and This Map

Artificial Intelligence (AI) patents represent inventions that utilize AI techniques, algorithms, or applications to solve specific problems. These can range from core machine learning models and neural network architectures to AI-driven systems in fields like healthcare, autonomous vehicles, natural language processing, and robotics.

Tracking AI patents provides insights into the pace of innovation, key technological trends, and the geographic distribution of research and development activity. Countries and companies leading in AI patent filings often indicate strong investment and focus in this transformative technology area.

About this map: This interactive map visualizes the estimated number of AI-related patent filings or grants associated with major innovation hubs around the world. The size of the circle is proportional to the number of patents (using a square root scale to moderate size differences). Click on a circle to see the estimated count for that region.

Disclaimer: The data presented here is purely illustrative and randomly generated for demonstration purposes. It does not reflect actual, up-to-date AI patent statistics. Real-world patent data is complex and requires rigorous analysis from specialized databases.
